Elkwood Construction Ltd. is a licensed pre-paid general contracting company serving Calgary and the province of Alberta. We are a full service company providing over 22 years of knowledge and expertise within the construction industry. Our speciality is high end residential renovations, basement developments, exterior decking and fencing. We have WCB and Liability Insurance coverage. We have experience dealing directly with Architects, Interior Designers, Engineers and directly with the homeowner.


The owner, Tom Searle, is a Red Seal Journeyman Carpenter and has developed an extensive network of sub-trades that take the same pride in their work as we do. This ensures the quality of craftsmanship and consistent level of finishing throughout the construction process. We deal directly with suppliers, manufacturers and wholesalers ensuring that you get the best price possible.
